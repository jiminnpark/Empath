Who we are

We are Team Dynamic Dudes providing AI-Solutions to Industries

Empath means expression of feelings and emotions.We have given this name to our project because it is strongly connected with feelings and emotions.This AI Assistant will help us to take feedback from user by taking their facial expression into consideration.

Empath has a base of Machine Learning.Face recognition,Landmarks detection models helped Empath to interact with Humans and based on several Machine learning ALgorithms Empath calculates the Feedback of User in the Backend.It learns fom Experience!

The idea came into mind of the creators when they had seen that nowadays Feedbacks aren't Honest enough and people give fake feebacks;also when Feedbacks are taken it takes much of the time being a manual work so We have thought of making all of this Automatic.Hence,Empath was born!
